Money on the move,
as live particles.

Draws the path of money — token → protocol → market/vault — across chains. Real on-chain transfers ride the edges as particles, so you see exactly which token is being deposited, swapped or withdrawn, where, right now.

Live · 30 min

The last 30 minutes (1 min delay) of real transfers flow as particles. Size = USD value (log), color = selected token, lanes = deposit / withdraw.

Baseline · 24h

Per-edge inflow/outflow averages over the last 24 hours show what “normal” looks like — so anomalies stand out against it.

Risk highlights

Nodes with oracle dependence, self-referential (NAV) pricing or depeg risk stand out by color and pulse. Unmeasurable nodes are marked, not hidden.

Honest data

We don't guess.

Every particle is a real on-chain transfer, and counterparties are resolved only through verified registries. Transfers that can't be resolved are never faked onto an edge.

  • Uniswap CREATE2 · Curve MetaRegistry · aToken · Comet · Morpho singleton
  • Unresolved transfers stay panel-only — never fabricated onto edges
  • Drag a node and connected nodes follow on a physics spring
One screen, two views

Flow + alerts.

On the main screen the flow map sits next to real-time monitoring alerts. Click an alert and the map jumps to a view filtered to just that token and chain.

  • Collapse the alert panel to expand the map
  • A live-status chip shows feed health at a glance
  • Multi-chain selection distinguishes the same token per chain
