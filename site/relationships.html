Relationship Map · token-centric rings + money-lego overlay

Money-lego overlay

Classifies derivative roles (PT, YT, LP, receipt, wrapper) so the chain — base → pool → derivative → re-deposit — stays unbroken.

Exposure concentration

How much of a token sits in which protocol, market or chain — pinpointing where a single point of failure could form.

Oracle dependence

Shows each market's oracle type (market, exchange-rate, NAV/self-referential). Pricing that can't catch a depeg is flagged in red.

Sinks & sources

Where it ends,
and where rewards come from.

Identifies “designed endpoints” — collateral locked, interest accrued, reward receipts, LP held — and shows, in one line, which yield a curator vault or YT is paid from.

  • Sink kinds: collateral · staking · reward · yield · LP · vault share
  • Exposure at the curator level (Morpho · Euler)
  • Back-traces upstream backing and underlying chains
Contagion paths

If one shakes,
how far does it reach?

The relationship map isn't a holdings list — it's a dependency graph. Gauge ahead of time how one token's depeg or liquidation propagates across derivatives, markets and vaults.

  • Identifies self-referential (ouroboros) markets
  • Shows cross-chain bridge mechanisms and their verification
  • Links to the same token & node as monitoring alerts
