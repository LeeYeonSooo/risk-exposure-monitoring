How it works

From verified data
to visible insight

Collect on-chain & aggregated data

Token exposure, transfers and market state from DeFiLlama, Alchemy, Morpho and on-chain RPC. Counterparties are resolved only via CREATE2, registries, aTokens, Comet and singletons.

Build the relationship & flow graph

Token ↔ protocol ↔ market ↔ vault, plus the bridges between chains, in one graph — derivative structures and oracle dependencies included.

Detect real-time flow & anomalies

Stream the last 30 minutes of real transfers as particles, and pick out anomalies from snapshot diffs. Compare against the 24h baseline to see what's unusual.

Alert → flow map → decision

The alert tells you “what happened”; the flow map shows “where and how”. Two views, one path to a fast decision.
